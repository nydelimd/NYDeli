NY Italian Deli Staff Time Clock

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Plain Language Summary

This app is a private employee time clock used by a single business. It collects only what is necessary to track employee work hours — employee names, PINs, and clock-in/out times. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with anyone outside the business. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time.

🏢 1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the NY Italian Deli Staff Time Clock iOS application ("the App"), operated by NY Italian Deli ("we," "us," or "our").

The App is a closed, business-internal tool used exclusively by authorized employees and administrators of NY Italian Deli. It is not a general-purpose consumer application.

📋 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the time clock system:

Data Type Purpose Collected?
Employee First & Last Name Identify employees in the system Yes
Numeric PIN (4 digits) Allow employees to clock in/out securely Yes
Clock-in / Clock-out Times Track work hours and generate reports Yes
Break Start / End Times Track break durations Yes
Admin Email Address Business owner login authentication Yes
Location Data Not applicable No
Photos / Camera Not applicable No
Contacts / Messages Not applicable No
Health Data Not applicable No
Advertising / Tracking IDs Not applicable No

⚡ We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial information, government IDs, biometric data, or any sensitive personal information.

🎯 3. How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Recording employee clock-in and clock-out events
  • Calculating total hours worked per employee per day
  • Displaying who is currently clocked in to authorized administrators
  • Generating work hours reports for payroll purposes
  • Authenticating the business administrator login
  • Syncing data across authorized business devices in real time

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the time clock system.

🔒 4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase Realtime Database, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest. Firebase is compliant with SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other industry security standards.

A copy of data is also stored locally on the device using Apple's UserDefaults system to allow the app to function while temporarily offline. This local data is protected by the device's built-in security features including device passcode and data encryption.

Access to data in Firebase is restricted to authenticated administrators only. Unauthorized access is not possible without valid login credentials.

🔐 All network communication between the app and Firebase is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We never transmit data over unencrypted connections.

🤝 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

We use the following third-party service solely to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — Cloud database and authentication provider. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Learn more at firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the safety and security of the business and its employees.

🗓️ 6. Data Retention

Employee records and clock-in/out data are retained for as long as the employee is active in the system or as required for payroll and labor law compliance purposes.

Business administrators can permanently delete any employee record and all associated time data at any time through the Admin Settings panel within the App.

Clock-in/out records may be retained for up to 3 years to comply with applicable labor and wage record-keeping requirements.

⚖️ 7. Your Rights

As an employee whose data is collected by this app, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data stored about you
  • Correction — Request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — Request that your data be deleted from the system
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format (JSON export is available through the admin panel)
  • Objection — Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the business administrator or reach out to us directly using the contact information below.

👶 8. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by employees who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor's data has been entered into the system, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

📱 9. Apple App Store and iOS

This App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own privacy policy, which is separate from this policy. We do not control Apple's data collection.

This App does not use Apple's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any cross-app tracking technologies. The App's tracking transparency declaration is: Not Tracked.

📝 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through the App or by other appropriate means.

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
